About Cardiac arrest

Sudden, unexpected loss of heart function, breathing and consciousness. In cardiac arrest, the heart abruptly stops beating. Without prompt intervention, it can result in the person's death. The main symptom is loss of consciousness and unresponsiveness. This medical emergency needs immediate CPR or use of a defibrillator. Hospital care includes drugs, an implantable device or other procedures.
